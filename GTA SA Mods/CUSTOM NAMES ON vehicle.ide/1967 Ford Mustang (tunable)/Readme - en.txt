Ford Mustang Shelby GT500 1967
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Mod on smart car - Ford Mustang Shelby GT500 1967 for GTA San Andreas. The model is made of high-resolution textures. Contains the paint work. The car also supports tuning. 

Features: 
- High quality textures; 
- Detailed salon; 
- Contains paint work; 
- Supports tuning; 
- Adaptation to IVF; 
- Own settings; 
- Supports all major functions of the game; 
- The car fits perfectly into the atmosphere of the game. 

You can download Ford Mustang Shelby GT500 1967 for GTA San Andreas the links at the bottom of the page. Install automatic. By default this mod is set to replace the Remington.
