Ford Focus Tuning
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Good mod on Ford Focus Tuning for GTA San Andreas. The model has as many as 3 paint work. Also has support for tuning. 

Features: 
- Good texture; 
- Contains paint work; 
- Supports tuning; 
- Own settings; 
- Supports all major functions of the game; 
- The car fits perfectly into the atmosphere of the game. 

Free download mod Ford Focus Tuning for GTA San Andreas with automatic installation using the links on this page. The mod is set to replace Flash.
