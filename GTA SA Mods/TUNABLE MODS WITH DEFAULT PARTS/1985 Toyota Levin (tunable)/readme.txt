Nissan Silvia Spec-R Aero (S15) '99
- Replaces Previon
- Functional front/rear lights textures
- Custom shadow and collision
- Tuningable at Transfender
- New extras added

Toyota Levin 85'
- Replaces Blistac
- Functional front/rear lights textures
- Custom shadow and collision
- Completely tuningable at Transfender
- New extras added

Nissan Skyline GT-R V (R-32) 93'
- Replaces Cadrona
- Functional front/rear lights textures
- New 3d front lights, doors (special thanx to Pumbars)
- Custom shadow and collision
- Tuningable at Transfender

1. Replace txd and dff files in gta3.img
2. Replace vehicle.txd in models/generic folder
