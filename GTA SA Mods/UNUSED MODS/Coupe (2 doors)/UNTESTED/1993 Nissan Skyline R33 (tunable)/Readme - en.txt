Nissan Skyline R33 Tunable
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Nissan Skyline R33 Tunable for GTA San Andreas. 

Features: 
- 2 tuning kit; 
- Doors, hood and trunk open; 
- A collision. 

Download модNissan Skyline R33 Tunable for GTA San Andreas, please follow the links below. By default, the mod is set in two clicks to replace Elegy.
