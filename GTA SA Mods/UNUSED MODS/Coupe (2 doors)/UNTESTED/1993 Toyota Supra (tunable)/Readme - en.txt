Toyota Supra US-Spec (JZA80) 1993 HQLM
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Toyota Supra US-Spec (JZA80) 1993 for GTA San Andreas. 

Features: 
- Quality envelope models from the game Juiced 2: Hot Import Nights 
- 2 extras 
- 2 tuning kit 
- 4 paintjobs (+ scan of the body to create your own vinyls) 
- Support of CA-Rooms and SA-colors 
- Support of own textures for burning lamps 
- Fully working: turn signals, brake lights, driving lights, fog lights, reverse 
- Fully working: steering wheel, speedometer, tachometer, odometer (!), wiper blades, brake pads 
- Fully working: the sensor of gasoline, oil, engine temperature; indicators low beam, turn signals in the cabin 
- High-quality illumination devices 
- Well customized fit player 
- Own Shadow, Conflicts, chassis_vlo 
- Realistic damage and management 
- Perfectly adapted to the fashion of the ENB series, and without it 
- Adapted to fashion "High Quality Light Mod 2.0" 
- NOT ADAPTED for play on servers in SAMP and MTA! 
- Replaces: JESTER
